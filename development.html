We used several tools and platforms to build and manage this project efficiently:
Trello helped us plan each week’s tasks, delegate responsibilities, and keep deadlines visible.
YouTube Studio allowed us to analyze audience behavior, adjust content type, and optimize publishing times.
Editing software like CapCut and Adobe Premiere Pro allowed us to craft a clear, engaging visual style.
Analytics Insights:
Our largest audience comes from smartphones — so we began prioritizing vertical-friendly and mobile-optimized content.
We noticed more engagement from female viewers, so we're working to diversify our content to attract male viewers too.
USA and European viewers made up the majority, guiding how we format titles, language, and timing.
